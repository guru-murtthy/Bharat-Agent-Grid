# Pitch Deck Outline — Bharat Agent Grid

> Use this as the script for the PDF pitch deck submission.

## 1. Title
Bharat Agent Grid — Trust-Verified, Voice-First Agentic Layer for Everyday India.

## 2. The problem (the gap)
Hundreds of millions of Indians are non-English, low-literacy, voice-first users
who cannot safely **act** through AI. Today's AI *talks* but does not *act with
verifiable trust*. This is the **last-mile trust + language + agency gap**.

## 3. Which challenge(s)
We deliberately connect **all three**: a technical agent grid (C1), a
trust-driven growth engine (C2), and a dignified everyday voice experience (C3).

## 4. The core solution
Speak your problem in your language. A grid of specialized agents completes the
real-life task, but a **Verifier** blocks anything not backed by a trusted
source, and a **Trust Ledger** keeps every step consented, auditable, and
reversible.

## 5. The user journey
1. Tap the mic, speak in Hindi/Kannada/English.
2. Planner understands and plans the task.
3. Agents act; grounding fetches verified sources.
4. Verifier approves / blocks / asks a human.
5. You approve by voice before any real action.
6. Action executes; you get a spoken confirmation + an undo option.
7. A proof-of-help card lets you help your family (referral loop).

## 6. Why it is novel (international)
- Trust Ledger as a first-class AI primitive.
- Action-grounded anti-hallucination.
- Voice-first for 20+ Indian languages.
- Trust-as-growth flywheel.

## 7. Growth & monetization (C2)
- Proof-of-help shareable cards + vernacular referral.
- Family delegation: one literate member onboards five others.
- Revenue: micro-fees on completed actions, B2B API for
  banks/hospitals/governments, verified-action premium tier.
